## Indirect Competitors

### Traditional Answering Services
- **Ruby Receptionists**, **AnswerConnect**, **PATLive**
- Human-only, expensive ($200-500+/month)
- Limited hours, no WhatsApp
- **Our advantage:** 24/7, 5x cheaper, AI + human option

### DIY Solutions
- **Google Voice** + **Calendly**
- Free but fragmented
- No AI, no WhatsApp automation
- **Our advantage:** All-in-one, intelligent

### Virtual Assistants
- **Belay**, **Time Etc**
- Human VAs, $1,500+/month
- General tasks, not receptionist-focused
- **Our advantage:** Specialized, 30x cheaper
