# Recepcionista.com - Executive Summary

## Product Vision

**Product:** An AI voice agent enabling service businesses to automate customer calls, WhatsApp interactions, and appointment bookings in Spanish/Portuguese. Combines conversational AI, realistic voice synthesis, and workflow automation.

**First Mover Advantage:** We will be the first to offer AI voice, WhatsApp, and human receptionists in the Spanish-speaking market.

---

## Mission

> Democratize AI-driven customer service for SMEs in Spain and Latin America, reducing operational costs by 40–60% while improving customer retention.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Spain's SME Sector
- **2.9M businesses** (86% of all companies)
- **72% in services** (healthcare, beauty, repair, hospitality)
- High labor costs, low tech adoption
- Pain point: Missing calls = lost revenue

### Latin America's WhatsApp Dominance
- **500M+ users**
- **92% of consumers** message businesses via WhatsApp
- WhatsApp is the primary communication channel (not email/phone)
- Huge opportunity for WhatsApp-first AI

## Key Differentiators

1. **WhatsApp-Native**
   - Not an afterthought—WhatsApp agent trained on same data as voice
   - Critical for LatAm market penetration

2. **Spanish-First**
   - Native Castilian Spanish (Spain) and Latin American variants
   - Cultural context understanding

3. **Simple Onboarding**
   - Add website → AI learns business in minutes
   - No complex setup or training required

4. **Realistic Voice**
   - ElevenLabs integration for natural-sounding AI
   - Multiple voice options per language/region

5. **Human Handoff**
   - Seamless transfer to human when needed
   - AI provides context to human agent
